# How to Stake $BFR?

## Step 1: Getting Started
If this is your first time on https://app.buffer.finance/, before you start placing up/down trades, follow the "Getting started" that will help you with connecting to the dApp, and preparing your wallet with sufficient ETH for gas among other pre-requisites.

## Step 2: Navigate to https://app.buffer.finance/#/earn

BFR: earns USDC rewards, esBFR, Multiplier Points when staked

To get started go to the **'BFR vault'** and click on **'Stake'**

<img width="1333" alt="Screenshot 2023-03-15 at 4 42 33 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/126849045/225292784-6f405cca-87ee-4778-8f5a-d02c80f29e06.png">

_Note if you don’t already have $BFR tokens (0x1A5B0aaF478bf1FDA7b934c76E7692D722982a6D) in your wallet, you can buy them on [Uniswap (Arbitrum)](https://app.uniswap.org/#/swap)._

Once you have $BFR tokens in your wallet, click on the “Stake” button present at the bottom of the vault card. 

## Step 3: Deposit BFR

Once you click on Stake, a pop-up will appear for entering the amount of BFR to be staked. Enter the amount, click on the “Stake” button, and confirm the transaction. 

<img width="351" alt="Screenshot 2023-03-15 at 4 47 09 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/126849045/225293814-63b4f722-bb95-4c6b-b3b6-a3798c3d02ac.png">

_Note: if this is your first time providing liquidity on Buffer, you will have to approve the smart contract to use your Buffer tokens before entering the amount and confirming the stake. To do that, click on the “Approve” button in the pop-up window._

The page will be automatically updated with the amount of your staked position, and a few seconds later, you will see the rewards start accumulating.

<img width="1231" alt="Screenshot 2023-03-15 at 4 58 05 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/126849045/225296192-7266555f-1130-442b-a69f-2951f0ee8f91.png">

#### Where does APR come from?
BFR Staking reward APR is denominated as esBFR APR + USDC APR. Staked BFR rewards APR is sourced from the binary options trading fees generated in a week. 

The APRs are updated weekly every Wednesday and will depend on the trading fees collected for the said week.

## Compound your rewards:
Compounding your rewards will stake your pending Multiplier Points and Escrowed BFR rewards, this will increase the amount of rewards you receive. Click on **'Compound'** on the **'Total Rewards'** card. A pop-up will appear, here right tick the rewards that you want to compound, and click **'Compound'** once more.

## Claim your rewards:
Under the 'Total Rewards' card lick on **'Claim'**

<img width="598" alt="Screenshot 2023-03-15 at 5 22 10 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/126849045/225301431-f028bec8-9b86-476b-99d7-6f4d7534e46e.png">

A window will appear, select the rewards you want to claim. To claim your BFR staking rewards - click on “Claim BFR Rewards.” Confirm the transaction.

## Unstake
Navigate to the BFR vault section and select “Unstake.”

<img width="616" alt="Screenshot 2023-03-15 at 5 26 08 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/126849045/225302290-164a3deb-8760-40ea-b307-4d1430aedf4f.png">

A pop-up will appear. Here input the number of vault shares you want to unstake and click on **'Unstake'** and confirm the transaction in your wallet.
